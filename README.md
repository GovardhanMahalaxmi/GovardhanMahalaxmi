## 💡 AI Engineering Principles I Live By

> **1. Design for observability first** — if you can't trace your RAG pipeline, you can't fix it.

> **2. Architecture decisions outlive models** — choose the right retrieval strategy, not just the latest model.

> **3. Agents need guardrails** — autonomous systems require human-in-the-loop checkpoints in production.

> **4. Data quality is AI quality** — garbage in, hallucination out. Ground every system in clean, structured knowledge.

> **5. PoC ≠ Production** — latency, cost, and reliability are first-class citizens from day one.
